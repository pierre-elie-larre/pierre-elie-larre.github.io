Master 2 internship proposal in theoretical quantum physics

Nonequilibrium dynamics in binary quantum fluids
The central aim of this master 2 internship is to construct a theoretical model addressing recent experimental observations regarding the far-from-equilibrium dynamics of two coupled quantum gases. The scope of engagement extends beyond this core project, offering significant latitude for expansion into the broader physics of binary quantum fluids.
